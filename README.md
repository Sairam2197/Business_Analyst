Overview:
This repository contains selected samples of work I have delivered as a Business Analyst across different projects and business scenarios.

Context:
The artefacts in this repository are designed to showcase my core competencies as a Business Analyst, including business process modelling, requirements documentation, data analysis, and stakeholder collaboration.

How to Review:
Each file in this repository represents a standalone sample and can be interpreted as work produced for real-world projects. The contents include:
BPMN diagrams illustrating end-to-end business processes
Process and requirements documents (BRD, FRD)
Dashboards showcasing analytical insights
JIRA screenshots reflecting requirements tracking and agile workflows
These samples collectively reflect my approach to understanding business needs, translating them into structured requirements, and supporting solution delivery.

Tools Used:
Figma – BPMN and process modelling
Google Docs – Business Requirement Documents (BRD) and Functional Requirement Documents (FRD)
Tableau – Dashboard creation and data visualisation
JIRA – Requirements management and agile project tracking

Skills Demonstrated:
Business process analysis and optimisation
Requirements elicitation and documentation
Data analysis and visualisation
Stakeholder communication
Agile and SDLC familiarity
